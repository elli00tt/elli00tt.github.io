Artist Statement

For this assignment we were tasked to convert our initials into animals and or plants using two different color harmonies. My initials are ELD. In the beginning, we started with a detailed pencil project where I excelled in my ability to get really detailed and use the value of lights and darks. For the second half of this assignment I chose the color harmonies of Warm Colors and Triadic Colors. My warm color piece came out very successful with everything popping and mixing extremely well with a clean finish. While my triadic piece was a lot more experimental, the neon colors were something very unique that many people loved.
