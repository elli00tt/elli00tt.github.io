Art Process & Color Theory

Art Process

  1. Sketch initials (ELD) into plant/animal form in pencil.
  2. Refine with shading and detail to emphasize value.
  3. Digitize the pencil drawing for further editing.
  4. Apply warm color harmony for strong contrasts.
  5. Experiment with triadic colors for a neon look.

Color Harmony Choices

  • Warm Colors: Red, orange, and yellow to create vibrancy and energy.
  • Triadic Colors: Balanced neon palette using three evenly spaced hues.
  • Contrast & Mood: Warm piece feels bold and structured, triadic piece feels playful and experimental.

Techniques Used

  • Pencil shading
  • Digital layering
  • Color harmony selection
